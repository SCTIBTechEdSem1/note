Syllabus

Module 1:
Introduction to instructional design
1. know about ID models
2. historical approaches of ID models
3. application of ID models
4. types of ID models
5. role of ID models in teaching and training

Module 2:
Instructional design models and their implications
1. explore motivational theory
2. design and develop a motivational session
3. explore ADDIE model
4. design and develop ADDIE based instructions
5. explore ASSURE model
6. design and develop ASSURE-based sessions
7. explore Merrill's First Principles of Instruction
8. design and develop Merrill's-based sessions

Module 3:
Planning and executing session
In this module, students will:
1. Gagne's Nine Events of Instruction
2. design and develop the lesson plan
3. develop the training package
4. explore TPACK model
5. explore SMART model

Module 4:
Develop the proper ID for training
In this module, students will:
1. develop and validate the ID models
2. integrate ID models
3. application of Kirkpatrick model in an educational evaluation
4. design and develop Self Dependent Learning Materials
